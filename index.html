FAQ

Clear boundaries before the first sprint.

What is NEXO?

NEXO is a prospect intelligence service by NEXOGREX S.R.L. We research and structure public-source local business data for U.S. agencies.

Do you send outreach campaigns?

Not as part of the core sprint. NEXO delivers research and structured prospect data. Your agency controls messaging, outreach tools, compliance, sending infrastructure, and follow-up.

Where does the data come from?

We use publicly available business information such as business websites, Google Business Profiles, public directories, and other visible online sources.

Are the leads guaranteed to book calls?

No. NEXO provides prospect intelligence, not guaranteed appointments or sales outcomes.

Can you avoid businesses we already contacted?

Yes, if you provide a suppression list, excluded domains, or existing account list before research begins.

How do payments work?

Payment is primarily by invoice and bank transfer. Other methods may be discussed depending on availability and client location.
