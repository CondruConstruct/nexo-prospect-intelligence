# NEXO Outreach Playbook

## Campaign goal

Validate which niche responds best by collecting 100 target companies in each category:

1. Web design agencies
2. Local SEO agencies
3. GoHighLevel agencies
4. PPC agencies
5. Reputation management agencies
6. Home-service marketing agencies

Do not send bulk email until the exact recipient list, sender identity, physical mailing address, unsubscribe wording, and template are approved.

## Prospect sources

Use public business/directories and manual review:

- LinkedIn search
- Clutch
- DesignRush
- UpCity
- GoodFirms
- Agency Spotter
- Sortlist
- Chamber/member directories
- Agency websites and public contact pages

## Qualification rules

Accept companies that:

- are U.S.-based or sell to U.S. local businesses;
- clearly sell SEO, web design, PPC, reputation, GoHighLevel, or home-service marketing;
- have a working website;
- have a public business email/contact form or LinkedIn path;
- appear active;
- likely have client value high enough to pay $350+.

Reject companies that:

- are consumer-only;
- have no clear service offer;
- look inactive/closed;
- sell risky/scam services;
- only look for ultra-cheap outsourced work;
- request private data or spam services.

## Positive reply handling

If they ask for details:

1. Ask target niche and geography.
2. Ask what service they sell.
3. Ask preferred fields.
4. Offer a $49–$99 paid sample or quote a $350+ Pilot Sprint.
5. Send scope confirmation.
6. Send invoice/payment instructions.
7. Start delivery only after payment/scope approval.

## Compliance guardrails

- Use truthful sender identity.
- Include physical mailing address for commercial email.
- Include clear opt-out wording.
- Maintain suppression/opt-out list.
- Do not send to opt-outs.
- Rate limit sending.
- Do not use deceptive subjects.
- Do not claim guaranteed meetings, compliance, or opt-in leads.
